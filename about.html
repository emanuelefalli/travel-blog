About

A quiet journal of distant places.

Wanderlines is a personal travel and photography journal, made slowly and without a schedule. The work here is the result of long walks, longer waits for the right light, and a stubborn preference for film over speed.

I am a photographer based in Berlin. I travel mostly alone, usually on foot, and I prefer landscapes that take a few days to reach. My camera is a small mirrorless body and one lens. I write because the photographs are not always enough.

Everything on this site was made by me. If you would like to use a photograph, or commission work, or simply say hello — write to me at hello@example.com.
